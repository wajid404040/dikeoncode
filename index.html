Week 4: Polish & Demo Preparation

Days 22-24: Creation of investor demo scenarios, addition of sample data for presentations, implementation of demo mode functionality, creation of presentation slides, and preparation of comprehensive technical documentation.

Days 25-28: Deployment to cloud platform, final testing and bug fixes, performance optimization, security audit, and completion of all documentation for investor presentations.
